Phone Motion Controls — No Touch Needed

All drum sounds are triggered by physically moving your phone. Do not tap the cards — just move the device.

Pitch Tilt forward = Kick  |  Tilt back = Snare
Roll Roll right = Hi-hat  |  Roll left = Crash
Yaw Swing right = Tom-1  |  Swing left = Tom-2

How to Play

Move phone, not screen. All sounds are triggered by device motion only — touching the cards does nothing.
Tilt forwardKick drum
Tilt backSnare
Roll rightHi-hat
Roll leftCrash cymbal
Swing rightTom-1
